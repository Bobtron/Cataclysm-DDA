12 /**
13  * Mod Tracking:
14  *
15  * These functions live here to provide various JSON-loaded entities
16  * with the ids of the mods that they are from.
17  *
18  * To subscribe, a JSON-loaded entity simply needs to have a
19  * 'std::vector<std::pair<(string or int)_id<T>, mod_id> src;' member.
20  * (As well as storing their id in an 'id' member of either string or int id type)
21  * If 'src' or 'id' is private, the subscriber must add 'mod_tracker' as a friend to access those members
22  *
23  * If the entity is loaded normally with generic_factory, no further changes are needed.
24  * If the entity is not loaded with generic_factory, 'assign_src()' must be called sometime
25  * after the 'id' member has been assigned.
26  */
