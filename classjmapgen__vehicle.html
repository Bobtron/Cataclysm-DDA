Detailed Description

Place a vehicle.

"vehicle": id of the vehicle. "chance": chance of spawning the vehicle: 0...100 "rotation": rotation of the vehicle, see fuel: fuel status of the vehicle, see status: overall (damage) status of the vehicle, see vehicle::vehicle
