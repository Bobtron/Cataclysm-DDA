Processes food and outputs nutrients that are finished processing Metabolic rates are required because they determine the rate of absorption of nutrients into the body.

All returned values are >= 0, with the exception of water, which can be negative in some circumstances (i.e. after eating dry/salty food). TODO: Update stomach capacity upon mutation changes, instead of calculating every time we need it, so we can get rid of 'owner' parameter here.
