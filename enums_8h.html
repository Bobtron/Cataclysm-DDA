Possible layers that a piece of clothing/armor can occupy.

Every piece of clothing occupies one distinct layer on the body-part that it covers. This is used for example by Character to calculate encumbrance values, player to calculate time to wear/remove the item, and by profession to place the characters' clothing in a sane order when starting the game.
