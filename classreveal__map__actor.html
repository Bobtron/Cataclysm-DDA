◆ radius

int reveal_map_actor::radius = 0

The radius of the overmap area that gets revealed.

This is in overmap terrain coordinates. A radius of 1 means all terrains directly around center are revealed. The center is location of nearest city defined in reveal_map_center_omt variable of activated item (or current player global omt location if variable is not set).
