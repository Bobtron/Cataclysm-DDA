◆ sleep_deprivation_levels

enum sleep_deprivation_levels

five levels of consequences for days without sleep

Sleep deprivation, distinct from sleepiness, is defined in minutes. Although most calculations scale linearly, malus is bestowed only upon reaching the tiers defined below.

Note
Sleep deprivation increases sleepiness. Sleepiness increase scales with the severity of sleep deprivation.
Sleep deprivation kicks in if lack of sleep is avoided with stimulants or otherwise for long periods of time
